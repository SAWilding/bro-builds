Budget FPV Drone Build

Building an FPV drone is not as difficult as you might first expect. There may be a few things such as soldering, that can dissuade someone from building their own drone. However, with a little practice you can become a soldering pro and slap together some amazing builds.

Here is a quick video tutorial on how to solder by electronhacks:

Here are the materials that I used for my own FPV drone build:

  • Emax Eco Series II Motors
  • Little Bee Spring 30-amp ESCs
  • Naze 32 Flight Controller
  • Basic PDB
  • Martian II 5 Inch Frame

As a good starting point, I would suggest that you watch this YouTube video by UAVfutures. It is a great resource for learning how to build a cheap and reliable drone.
